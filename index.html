About Me

I've been an advocate of real estate for over a Decade & a Half in Hyderabad with elaborate exposure & expertise in Marketing, Sales, Designing of Brand Strategy, Leadership Mentoring, & Go-To-Market Strategies. As a Licensed RERA Realtor & a member of HRA & NAR India, I have distinguished myself in the industry as a Specialist who builds Eco Systems for my Clients in the Real Estate Industry.

With an MBA in International Business from California, USA, my strong academic background enhances my credibility in the field. I am known for optimizing costs and maximizing profits while maintaining a deep commitment to client success.

In addition to my work in real estate, I mentor startups as well as established organisations in their attempt to Scale & Tackle Business Challenges. My Penchant for wanting to better the Real Estate Industry, sets me apart as a trusted figure in Hyderabad's real estate market.
